Frequently asked

  • Do I need an account? No. You can play right away. Signing in with Google or Apple is optional and only used to back up your records.
  • How do I unlock Blind mode? Reach the required max combo in Combo mode, and Blind mode unlocks automatically.
  • How do I delete my data? Open the app → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated records.
